A Clearer Vision
GELIT FINALS PROJECT

Rob Kjelle Gabrielle

L. Mabilangan

This motion graphics video invites viewers to reimagine a perfect world through the lens of unity and acceptance. The journey begins with a close-up of an eye, symbolizing perspective and reflection. As the camera zooms inward, it shows a world with vibrant symbols of hope, connection, and harmony. Through powerful visuals and a message of inclusion, the video challenges us to see beyond divisions and envision a brighter, more unified future.
