All Eye Seeing

June U. Platon

My Artwork

This artwork shows an eye that everyone dreams of a perfect world, a peaceful sky with fluffy clouds. But hidden within this dream, a dark, tangled thought takes shape. It is a reminder that even in our purest intentions, we can harbor hidden judgments. This artwork encourages us to look beyond appearances and to understand that everyone, including ourselves, has both light and shadow within.

Made by June Platon.
