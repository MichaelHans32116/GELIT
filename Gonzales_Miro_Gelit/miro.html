A World of Possibilities

A vision of hope and harmony

In a world where spirits soar,
There will be no chains of indecency forevermore.
A different vision of painted hues,
Where every soul seeks a peace of balance, true.

A symphonic and hopeful sound,
Represents the heart, where dreams are found.
Where all the love comes to sing,
On sacred grounds, where hope takes wing.

No walls shall break the boundaries wide,
Of judgment cast or love denied.
For in this place, compassion reigns,
A refuge built from joy, not pain.

So let's dream a world renewed in light,
Where empathy blooms, erasing the night.
A garden of care, both strong and true,
That binds together me and you.

A tapestry woven of vibrant threads,
Where courage and kindness are widespread.
No shadows linger, no fear takes hold,
A future of promise, bright and bold.

The stars above will softly glow,
Guiding paths where dreams will grow.
United hearts in harmony sing,
A melody carried on endless wings.

Let us build this world with every hand,
A sacred and cherished promised land.
Where hope ignites the darkest skies,
And love forever fills our lives.
